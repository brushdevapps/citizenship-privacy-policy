Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 18, 2026
Brush Apps ("we", "our", or "us") operates the US Citizenship Test 2025 mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.
Information We Collect
Information Stored Locally on Your Device
The App stores the following data locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers:
Study progress and statistics (questions studied, streak, XP)
Bookmarked questions
Test session history and scores
App preferences (theme, test version, settings)
Premium license status
Information Collected by Third Parties
The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:
Google AdMob
The free version of the App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. This may include:
Device identifiers (advertising ID)
IP address
General location (country/region level)
App usage data related to ad interactions
Premium users do not see ads and are not subject to AdMob data collection within the App.
For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses data from apps that use their services.
Information We Do Not Collect
We do not collect, store, or transmit:
Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
Location data
Contacts or photos
Account or login credentials (the App does not require an account)
Any study data or progress — all data stays on your device
Data Storage and Security
All user data (progress, settings, bookmarks) is stored locally on your device using Android's standard data storage mechanisms. We do not operate servers that receive or store your personal data. If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data is deleted.
Children's Privacy
The App is an educational study tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not require account creation or submission of any personal data.
Third-Party Links
The App may reference external resources such as uscis.gov for official study materials. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.
Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
